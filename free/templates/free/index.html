Elab Free

Welcome to the new elab platform.
The e-lab is a space where real experiments can be carried out via the Internet. The experiments are physically assembled and installed in a laboratory of the Instituto Superior Tecnico or in partner science schools and centers. The experience is controlled by its administrator, who is no more than the first member of the waiting list of users interested in carrying it out. There is also a list that allows, for example, a teacher to carry out the experiment and his students to receive the image and the data at the same time, even though they may be in physically distant locations.

The data from the experiments and the image of the events (videos) are captured through sensors connected, directly or indirectly, to a central computer, from where they are broadcast via the Internet. Each control room , which corresponds to a particular experiment, has its own space, where an experimental protocol, suggestions for variants of the experiment, as well as explanations and data analysis are suggested.
Control room, experimental apparatus, protocol and experimental setup are key concepts in e-lab:

  • Control room virtual environment for controlling a real experimental apparatus included in a given laboratory.
  • Experimental apparatus equipment that allows carrying out a certain experiment.
  • Experimental Setup configuration of the experimental apparatus according to the protocol to be performed.
  • Protocol sequence of experiment execution with the respective selection and configuration of the experimental setup.
